About us

welcome to गृहseva! A place where all your household needs are met. Our main idea is to help homemakers in their daily tasks and giving them a chance to feel free. Our services are divided into two categories, one being reccurring and the other one being the one time services. Here the reoccurring services are the services which the user can continue for a specific time period according to their wish, whereas one time services are only available for a day. Some of the reoccurring services includes maid, cook help, nanny, security guard, gardner.
